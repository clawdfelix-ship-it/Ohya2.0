# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 24, 2026

This privacy policy outlines how Mzakka E-commerce ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data in accordance with the **Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Chapter 486)** of Hong Kong ("PDPO").

## 1. Personal Data Collection Statement

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Register an account on our website
- Place an order for products or services
- Subscribe to our newsletter or marketing communications
- Contact us for customer support
- Complete online forms or surveys

The types of personal data we may collect include:
- Name, contact information (email address, phone number, shipping address)
- Billing information and payment details
- Account login credentials
- Order history and purchase preferences
- Communication records with our customer service team

## 2. Purpose of Collection

We collect and use your personal data for the following purposes:
- Processing and fulfilling your orders, including delivery and payment processing
- Verifying your identity for account security and access control
- Providing customer support and responding to your inquiries
- Processing returns and after-sales services
- Sending important notifications about your orders, account status, or service changes
- Marketing communications (only with your explicit consent), including promotional offers and updates
- Improving our website, products, and customer experience
- Complying with legal and regulatory requirements in Hong Kong

We will not collect or use your personal data for any purpose other than what is stated in this policy without your prior consent.

## 3. User Rights Under PDPO

Under the PDPO, you have the following rights:

- **Right of Access:** You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to Correction:** You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** You may withdraw your consent for us to use your personal data for marketing purposes at any time.
- **Right to Data Portability:** Where applicable, you may request a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.
- **Right to Complain:** If you believe we have violated your rights under the PDPO, you may file a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data in Hong Kong.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@mzakka.com.hk.

## 4. Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal data in accordance with legal requirements in Hong Kong:

- **Transactional records (invoices, orders, payment records):** Retained for **7 years** after the completion of the transaction, as required by the Inland Revenue Ordinance (Chapter 112) of Hong Kong.
- **Account data:** Retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.
- **Marketing communications data:** Retained until you withdraw your consent.
- **Logs and security records:** Retained for 12 months for security and audit purposes.

After the retention period expires, we will securely delete or anonymize your personal data in accordance with our data security procedures.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:
- Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
- Access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Secure data deletion procedures

## 6. Disclosure of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data to third parties except as described below:
- **Service Providers:** We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, delivering orders, or conducting our business (e.g., payment processors, logistics providers, hosting services).
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your personal data if required by law, court order, or government regulation in Hong Kong.
- **Business Transfer:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our business, your personal data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest version will be posted on our website with the updated effective date.
